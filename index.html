About CartLu

CartLu is an innovative platform designed to empower local fruit and vegetable hawkers by providing them with a digital presence and connecting them directly with customers in their area.

Our mission is to support small businesses, reduce food waste, and ensure everyone has access to fresh, high-quality produce.

How It Works

Select Produce

Browse fresh fruits and vegetables offered by local hawkers.

Place Order

Add your chosen items to the cart and place your order.

Get Delivery

Our partners deliver fresh produce right to your doorstep.
